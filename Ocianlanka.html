In 2023, Ocian Lanka made significant strides in its sustainability initiatives, exemplifying its commitment to environmental responsibility and waste management. This recycling rate underscores Ocian Lanka's dedication to fostering a circular economy, where waste is minimized, and resources are reused.



Moreover, Ocian Lanka achieved an impressive reuse rate of 70%, indicating that a significant portion of its waste materials were given a second life. This high reuse percentage highlights the company's innovative strategies and effective waste management practices, which focus on maximizing the utility of materials and reducing the need for virgin resources. This figure represents the portion of waste that could not be recycled or reused and thus was disposed of. While this indicates room for improvement, it also provides a clear benchmark for future sustainability goals and initiatives.



Overall, Ocian Lanka's accomplishments in 2023 reflect a proactive and forward-thinking approach to waste management. The significant percentages of recycled and reused materials demonstrate the company's commitment to sustainability and its ongoing efforts to reduce environmental impact. These achievements set a strong foundation for future initiatives aimed at further improving waste management and enhancing environmental stewardship.
